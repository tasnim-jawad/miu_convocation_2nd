Massage from PM

Hon’ble Chairman, BOT, MIU Manarat International University is a unique academic institute which has different set of visions. We established MIU in order to provide quality education and instill moral values to the students and thereby make them educated future leaders with high moral standards. The Board of Trustees has no profit motive and no one takes a single penny of profit from this institute. Our sole goal is to serve the humanity. By the grace of Almighty Allah, our university is gradually moving towards fulfilling the hopes and dreams of our nation by creating skilled manpower and the required leadership qualities. May Allah grant MIU success so that it can produce worthy citizens for the cause of mankind.

Sheikh Hasina
